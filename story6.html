Elaina Calls Group “Dirty Filthy No Manner Havin’ Yankees”

Elaina Escalates Manners Debate With New Insult

What began as a simple conversation about manners spiraled quickly this evening after Elaina expressed frustration at the group’s behavior. When someone commented that people “apparently don’t” have manners, Elaina responded with:

“Apparently y’all don’t.”

Moments later, she followed it up with a far more explosive line:

“Dirty filthy no manner havin’ Yankees.”

The group reacted instantly, with several users stunned at the sudden escalation. Some took it as a joke. Others weren’t sure. And a few insisted they had “plenty of manners actually.”

Whether this becomes the next major server-wide debate or just another memorable Elaina moment is still unclear, but one thing’s certain — the phrase has already taken on a life of its own in chats and DMs.
